About the Game

A game for anyone who's tired of making real donuts. Now you can make virtual donuts with simple mouse clicks!
Just click the cartoon donut to get started. As you collect more donuts, you'll unlock the ability to purchase auto-clickers and donut multipliers with the donuts you've made (it takes donuts to make donuts?).
Auto-clickers will make donuts for you each second. One auto-clicker will add one donut per second, two auto-clickers will add 2 donuts per second, etc.
Donut multipliers will increase the number of donuts made with each click (auto-clicks included). Each multiplier increases the value of a click by 20%.
You'll need more donuts to purchase each auto-clicker or multiplier. Naturally.
